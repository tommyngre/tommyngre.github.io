About Me

Woah, you made it to my page! I'm Tommy.

I grew up in Chicago and its northern suburbs, and graduated from University of Wisconsin (Madison) in 2007. Since then, I've mostly worked in healthcare IT. For about 6 years, I worked for Epic, an enterprise healthcare softare vendor in Verona, Wisconsin. Currently, I work at LabCorp, a clinical laboratory network in Burlington, North Carolina, where I certify reverse reference interfaces.
